Tidal Goods Co.
🛍️ Retail & Merchandising
Problems You'll Solve
  • Calculate COGS using FIFO & weighted average
  • Set selling prices to hit a target markup %
  • Model markdown strategies for slow-moving inventory
  • Apply trade & cash discount chains to purchase orders
  • Track inventory levels and reorder points
About the Company

A boutique retail business selling lifestyle goods through direct-to-consumer and wholesale channels. One owner, no finance team — every dollar runs through a spreadsheet.

Course Modules
Inventory COGS Markup & Markdown Discounts

Retail math is everywhere — every store, every buyer, every supplier negotiation. Tidal Goods gives you the math behind the price tag.

Anchor & Oak Events
🎪 Events & Hospitality
Problems You'll Solve
  • Calculate the breakeven number of events to cover fixed costs
  • Model 12-month seasonal cash flow
  • Determine labor cost per event with variable hours & crew
  • Build pricing models that guarantee margin on every package
  • Analyze how event mix affects overall profitability
About the Company

An event planning company booking weddings, corporate events, and seasonal gatherings. Revenue is lumpy — a great summer subsidizes a quiet winter. Cash flow and breakeven are survival skills here.

Course Modules
Breakeven Cash Flow Labor Costs Pricing Strategy

Anchor & Oak teaches you to think like an owner — before you sign the lease, hire the staff, or quote the price.

Harborside Medical Center
🏥 Healthcare
Problems You'll Solve
  • Calculate payer mix % to identify revenue by insurance type
  • Process payroll with shift differentials & overtime
  • Analyze budget variance: actual vs. budgeted by department
  • Build charts that turn large datasets into executive visuals
  • Apply % change calculations to track performance over time
About the Company

A regional healthcare organization serving patients across multiple insurance plans. Revenue depends not just on services delivered, but on who is paying and at what reimbursement rate.

Course Modules
Data Analysis Payer Mix % Payroll Revenue Reporting

Healthcare is the largest employer in most U.S. regions. Payer mix, payroll, and variance analysis transfer directly to health management and hospital finance careers.
