## 📖 Reading: Why Data Matters
Before we jump into the spreadsheet, it is important to understand that **data is only as good as its organization.** In this module, we are focusing on **Clean Data**. Clean data means:
* No empty rows in the middle of your dataset.
* Consistent formatting (don't mix "10%" and "0.1").
* Clear headers for every column.

> **Key Concept:** Always keep a "Raw Data" tab that you never edit, and do your work on a "Calculation" tab. This prevents you from losing your original information!
