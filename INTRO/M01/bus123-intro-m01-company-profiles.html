Inventory COGS Markup & Markdown Discounts

Tidal Goods Co. is a boutique retail business selling lifestyle goods through both direct-to-consumer and wholesale channels. The owner manages purchasing, inventory, and pricing without a dedicated finance team — meaning every dollar in and out runs through a spreadsheet.

The Business Problems You Will Solve
  • Calculate cost of goods sold (COGS) using FIFO and weighted average
  • Determine the correct selling price to hit a target markup percentage
  • Model a markdown strategy when seasonal inventory isn't moving
  • Track inventory levels and reorder points in a live Excel workbook
  • Apply trade and cash discount chains to wholesale purchase orders
Why This Company

Retail math is everywhere — every store, every buyer, every supplier negotiation. Tidal Goods gives you a realistic context for the math behind the price tag you see every time you shop.

💼 Financial Services

Payroll Time Value of Money Interest & Loans Taxes

Meridian Advisory Group is a financial services and consulting firm. The team manages client portfolios, processes internal payroll, and produces analytical reports where accuracy is non-negotiable — a single formula error can mean a client receives the wrong advice or the wrong paycheck.

The Business Problems You Will Solve
  • Process payroll for employees with different pay rates and withholding structures
  • Calculate the present and future value of client investment scenarios using Excel TVM functions
  • Build loan amortization schedules showing principal, interest, and balance over time
  • Apply federal income tax brackets to determine a client's tax liability
  • Model the impact of interest rates on borrowing costs
Why This Company

Finance careers live inside spreadsheets. Meridian puts you in the role of the analyst — not just reading numbers, but building the models that produce them.

Company Profile: Anchor & Oak Events

You will see this icon on Anchor & Oak
worksheets & examples
🎪 Events & Hospitality

Breakeven Cash Flow Labor Costs Pricing Strategy

Anchor & Oak Events is an event planning and production company that books weddings, corporate events, and seasonal gatherings. Revenue is lumpy — a great summer can subsidize a quiet winter — which means cash flow management and breakeven analysis are survival skills, not optional extras.

The Business Problems You Will Solve
  • Calculate the breakeven number of events needed to cover fixed and variable costs
  • Model 12-month seasonal cash flow to identify when cash shortfalls will occur
  • Determine labor cost per event when crew size and hours vary
  • Build a pricing model that ensures every package covers costs and generates margin
  • Analyze how changing event mix affects overall profitability
Why This Company

Service businesses live and die by breakeven math. Anchor & Oak teaches you to think like an owner — before you sign the lease, before you hire the staff, before you quote the price.

Data Analysis Payer Mix % Payroll Revenue Reporting

Harborside Medical Center is a regional healthcare organization serving patients across multiple insurance plans and payer types. Revenue analysis here is more complex than retail — what the center earns depends not just on services delivered, but on who is paying and at what reimbursement rate.

The Business Problems You Will Solve
  • Calculate payer mix percentages to understand which insurance plans drive the most revenue
  • Process payroll with shift differentials, overtime, and multiple pay classifications
  • Analyze departmental budget variances using actual vs. budgeted comparisons
  • Build data visualizations that turn large datasets into executive-ready charts
  • Apply percentage change calculations to track performance over time
Why This Company

Healthcare is the largest employer in most U.S. regions. The analytical skills Harborside requires — payer mix, payroll, variance — transfer directly to hospital administration, health management, and insurance careers.
