BUS123 · Math M08 · Cash Flow Timeline

Move one cash flow through time.

Students decide whether a cash flow is moving earlier or later, then connect that direction to present value or future value logic before building the Excel formula.

Set the Move

Start from the workbook-style question: what is a cash flow worth in a different year?

PV or FV?

A $250 cash flow in year 15 is being moved back to year 3.

Direction Earlier
Periods Moved 12
Value at Target Year $64.17
Original Year 15 · $250
Target Year 3 · $64.17

Excel Translation

Because the cash flow moves earlier, discount it with PV.

=PV(rate, periods, 0, -future_value)

Math Logic

Divide by the growth factor for each period you move backward.

Target Value = Cash Flow / (1 + rate)^periods
Closing Question

When comparing two offers, why is it unfair to compare cash flows that happen in different years without moving them to the same point in time?
