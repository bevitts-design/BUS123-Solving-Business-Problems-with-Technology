Overtime Adds a Premium

Regular pay = regular hours × rate
Overtime pay = overtime hours × rate × 1.5
=MIN(Hours,40)*Rate + MAX(Hours-40,0)*Rate*1.5
40 hours is the hinge
Scheduling Decision

One Person at 52 Hours or Two People at 26?

Overtime can solve a short staffing problem.
It can also hide a headcount problem.
High overtime: fast coverage, higher marginal cost.
More staff: lower overtime, more fixed scheduling complexity.

Nurse Coverage Week

Not Cash Leaving Today

Depreciation estimates the asset cost used up in each accounting period.
Book value = cost − accumulated depreciation.

Straight-Line Method

Why Managers Like It

Predictable expense each year.
Easy to explain and audit.
Useful when an asset provides steady service.
Simple schedule for budgets and forecasts.
cost starts high
book value falls evenly
Worked Example

Ultrasound Unit

Cost: $84,000
Residual value: $12,000
Useful life: 6 years
($84,000 - $12,000) ÷ 6 = $12,000 per year
Annual depreciation$12K

The same expense repeats while the equipment provides steady service.
