Chart polish checklist

Default Excel charts are drafts

Title

Say what the chart shows, not just “Chart 1.”

Labels

Axis labels or data labels where a reader needs units.

Clutter

Remove extra gridlines, borders, and unused legends.

Color

Use color to focus attention, not decorate every bar.

Common Mistake

Summarizing a messy range and trusting the output

What goes wrong

A blank row, manual total, or unrefreshed PivotTable makes the chart or summary quietly wrong.

Better move

Use an Excel Table, keep raw data row-by-row, and run Refresh All before presenting.
