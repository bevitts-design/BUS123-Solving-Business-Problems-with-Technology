BUS12320

Every Semester, Same Mistake

⚠ COMMON MISTAKE
Submitting the Wrong File
Uploading a blank starter template or last week's homework is not recoverable after the deadline. Before hitting submit, check two things: (1) the file name matches the assignment, and (2) open the file and confirm you're looking at your work.
