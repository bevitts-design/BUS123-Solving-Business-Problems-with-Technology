Common Mistake
⚠ Every semester
Saving to Desktop instead of OneDrive.

If it's not in OneDrive, it doesn't exist.

Files saved locally get lost when laptops have problems — and they do. OneDrive is your safety net. Get in the habit now. "My computer crashed" is not an explanation we accept.

What is a business problem?

Group Activity · 10 minutes
Your question
What is a business problem?
Pick a real business — a store, restaurant, streaming service, or airline.
Name one specific problem they had to solve to deliver what you paid for.
Be specific. Not "supply chain issue" — say what went wrong and why.
Groups of 3
Turn to the people near you. One example per group. We'll share out after ten minutes.
Tip
The more specific you are, the more useful the analysis. Vague problems get vague solutions.
A useful frame

Most business problems fit one of three buckets.

Information
They don't know something.
Missing data, no visibility into inventory, unclear customer behavior. The problem is a knowledge gap.
Coordination
People aren't working together.
Departments out of sync, duplicate work, decisions made without the right people.
Efficiency
Something costs too much or takes too long.
A process that should take an hour takes a week. The right tool cuts it down.
Share out

Let's hear what your groups found.

Question 01
What was the business problem your group chose — and which bucket does it fall into?
Question 02
